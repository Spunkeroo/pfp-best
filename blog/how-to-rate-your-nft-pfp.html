How to Get Your NFT PFP Rated by the Community

You've got an NFT PFP. Maybe it's a Bitcoin Ordinal, an Ethereum blue-chip, or a Solana generative piece. But how do you actually know if it's a banger — or if you need to upgrade?

The only real answer comes from the community. That's why pfp.best exists: a free, no-login platform where real people rate NFT profile pictures 1–10. Here's exactly how to use it.

Key fact: Most NFT holders never get honest feedback on their PFP. pfp.best gives you a real community score — plus an AI rating — so you know exactly where you stand.

Step-by-Step: Rate Your NFT PFP

1

Go to pfp.best and upload your PFP

Visit pfp.best. Tap the upload zone and select your PFP image from your device. Or drag & drop it directly. No wallet connection required — just the image file.

2

Select your blockchain

Choose Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Solana (SOL), or Art Only. This tells the community which chain your PFP is from and puts it in the right leaderboard category.

3

Rate your own PFP first

As soon as you upload, your PFP modal opens. Give it a rating 1–10 to kick off your score. This is important — it starts the algorithm and shows other users you're engaged.

4

Get the AI rating

Hit "🤖 Get AI Rating" in the modal. Our on-device AI (powered by MobileNet) analyzes your PFP's visual properties and gives it a score with a roast. Totally free, totally honest.

5

Share on X to get more votes

Use the "𝕏 Share on X" button to post your PFP rating. Every time someone clicks your link and votes, your community score updates in real time. The more votes, the more accurate your rating.

6

Check the leaderboard

Filter by BTC, ETH, or SOL on the leaderboard to see where your PFP ranks globally. Top-rated PFPs get featured on the homepage.

Why Community Ratings Matter More Than Floor Price

In 2024–2025, the PFP meta shifted. Owning a high-floor NFT no longer automatically means a good profile picture. The community has become more discerning:

  • A 0.1 ETH PFP with great composition beats a 5 ETH PFP that looks bad at thumbnail size
  • Community recognition (votes, shares, comments) builds your on-chain reputation
  • Viral PFPs get shared more — creating secondary demand for the underlying NFT
  • Platform algorithms favor profile pictures with high engagement rates

A high rating on pfp.best tells you your PFP is actually working for you — not just sitting in your wallet.

What Makes an NFT PFP Score High?

After analyzing thousands of community ratings, here's what consistently scores 8+/10 on pfp.best:

  • Readable at small size — Clear at 48px × 48px, not just at full resolution
  • High contrast — Dark/light contrast makes it pop against any background
  • Distinctive character — Something immediately recognizable and memorable
  • Emotional resonance — Makes people feel something (cool, funny, powerful, cute)
  • Clean background — Busy backgrounds hurt small-size readability

How the Rating System Works

pfp.best uses a transparent scoring system:

  • Star ratings (1–10) — Each user rates once per PFP. The average becomes the PFP's rating score.
  • Upvotes / Downvotes — Quick voting for users who don't want to assign a specific number
  • Comments — The roast section. Honest feedback from the community
  • Score algorithm — Combines rating average, vote balance, and recency for leaderboard position
  • AI rating — An independent computer vision score that runs in your browser

Tips to Boost Your PFP's Rating

Use the Referral System

Share your unique referral link (visible on the pfp.best homepage). Every friend who uploads a PFP through your link gives your submissions a +5 vote boost. Build your network, boost your score.

Engage with Other PFPs

Rate other people's PFPs honestly. Drop comments. Vote. The pfp.best community is built on reciprocity — the more you engage, the more engagement you get back.

Get the Timing Right

Upload during peak hours for your target community. Bitcoin holders are most active during US morning and European afternoon. Ethereum and Solana communities overlap with global crypto Twitter hours (roughly 13:00–21:00 UTC).

Cross-Post Your Rating

When you get a great rating (8+/10), share it everywhere — your NFT collection's Discord, your Twitter profile, relevant crypto subreddits. A pfp.best rating is social proof that your PFP is actually respected.

What If Your PFP Scores Low?

A low score is valuable information, not a personal attack. If your PFP scores below 6/10, consider:

  • Is the image resolution too low? Upscale with Adobe Firefly or similar tools
  • Does it look bad at thumbnail size? Consider cropping to the focal point
  • Is it visually interesting? Commission a custom PFP on Fiverr
  • Want something totally unique? Generate AI PFPs with Midjourney

Read the comments on your PFP. They're usually honest. The community tells you exactly what to fix.
